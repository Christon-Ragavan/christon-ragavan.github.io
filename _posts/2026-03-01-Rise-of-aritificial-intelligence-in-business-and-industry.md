# Introductions

- Emerging technologies always reshapes teh business landscape, one such technology distributing of this era is AI 
- AI systems can have different types of compositions; Natual language processing, knowledge representations,automated reasoning, actionable automations (AI agents), RAG systems etc.
- It is important to synthesis the knowledge before identifying the trends and potential business gaps
- Applications are wide across industries

  - Manufacturing
